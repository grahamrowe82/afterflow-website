Frequently Asked Questions

When does the beta start?
We'll onboard our first beta users by April 2025. Sign up now to reserve your spot and receive early access benefits.
How does Slack integration work?
Seamlessly connect Slack from your Afterflow dashboard in just a few clicks. Afterflow will instantly send reminders, task updates, and notifications directly into your Slack workspace—no complex setup needed.
Is there a free plan?
Absolutely! Afterflow offers a generous Free Tier so you can fully experience our core features, tracking up to 20 meetings per month without charge. Upgrade whenever you’re ready for unlimited coverage and advanced AI insights.
What about data privacy and security?
Your data is yours alone—we never share or sell your information. Afterflow adheres to industry-standard security practices, and your meeting data is encrypted and securely stored. For complete details, please view our Privacy Policy.
What features are included in the Free Tier?
The Free Tier includes persistent task tracking across meetings, real-time Slack reminders, and basic monthly analytics. It’s perfect for individual use or small teams.
Can I invite teammates to Afterflow?
Yes! Inviting teammates is easy and encouraged. Afterflow becomes even more powerful when your whole team is aligned. Share your invite link directly from your dashboard.
How easy is it to upgrade to the Plus or Pro plan?
Upgrading is simple—no complex processes or hidden fees. Just visit your dashboard and select “Upgrade” anytime to unlock unlimited meetings (Plus) or unlimited meetings plus advanced AI analytics (Pro). Choose whichever fits your needs, and easily switch between plans at any time.
Can I cancel or downgrade my plan later?
Yes, you're never locked in. Easily downgrade or cancel directly from your account settings with no questions asked.
